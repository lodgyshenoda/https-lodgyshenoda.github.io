About Me

I'm Lodgy Shenouda, an incredibly ambitious and persistent individual. My passion for graphic design started back in the second year of middle school when I discovered how powerful design can be as a way to express ideas and emotions. Since then, I haven't stopped learning and improving my skills, and with every step forward, I've been driven to keep going.

Although I'm currently a first-year medical student, my love for graphic design remains unchanged. I'm still dedicated to learning and growing in this field, and I truly believe that no matter the challenges, design will always be a part of my life.

"Lily's Design" is the place where I share my passion and creativity with you, and every design is a part of my journey.
